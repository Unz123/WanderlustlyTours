Punjab, PAKISTAN - $125

Lahore is a historic and cultural city located in the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is known for its magnificent Mughal-era architecture, delicious food, and vibrant markets. One of the most famous attractions in Lahore is the Badshahi Mosque, which is one of the largest mosques in the world and a masterpiece of Mughal architecture. The Lahore Fort, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is also a must-visit for tourists interested in history and culture.It is also known for its colorful festivals, such as the Basant Kite, Lahore Literary Festival, which attract visitors from all over the world.
